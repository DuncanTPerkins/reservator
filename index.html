Instructions

Hi there! This is my project for Math for Computer Science. It's a matrix multiplier that I wrote (I promise) using Angular JS, HTML, and two lines of JQuery! To operate it, put numbers into the two matrixes above the horizontal line, and the matrixes on the bottom will fill up with numbers. The Addition matrix will start filling up right away, but the Multiplication matrix will take a few fields of input, since each field is dependent on more than 2. Enjoy!
